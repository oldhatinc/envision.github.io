EnVision 2.0.1 is an interactive engine for RPG-style scenarios, functioning as the game master. Users play characters, influencing the story through decisions and actions.

About

EnVision is designed to provide a dynamic and customizable gameplay experience, ensuring a captivating narrative flow with user-driven interactions. Our system is tailored to offer:

  • Comprehensive game master functionalities to guide the narrative.
  • Customizable scenarios and narratives to suit player preferences.
  • Dynamic interaction management that responds to player decisions.
  • Enhanced storytelling capabilities to create immersive experiences.

With EnVision, every adventure is unique, driven by the players' choices and actions, making each session an unforgettable journey.

Features

  • Interactive Storytelling: Tailor stories based on player choices, ensuring a personalized narrative experience.
  • User Profiles: Store player preferences, past interactions, and progress for a seamless experience.
  • Dynamic Weight Adjustment: Adjust game elements dynamically based on user feedback and interactions.
  • Scenario Templates: Access a library of pre-made scenarios, ready to be customized and played.
  • Performance Metrics: Track and display success rates and completion times to monitor progress.
  • Advanced Narrative Control: Manage branching storylines and conditional logic to create complex narratives.
  • Visual References: Request visual references to enhance the scenario experience and immersion.
  • Dynamic Creativity: Enhance narrative depth, engagement, and immersion with creative elements.
